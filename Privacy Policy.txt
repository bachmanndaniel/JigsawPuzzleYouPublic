Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 January 2026

This application (“Jigsaw Puzzle YOU”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All features of the app function entirely on your device.

Photos and Media

If you choose to create puzzles from images on your device:

Images are accessed only after your explicit permission

Images are used solely within the app to generate puzzles

Images are not uploaded, transmitted, backed up, or shared

Images never leave your device

The app does not retain copies of your images outside of normal local app usage.

Permissions

The app may request access to media or images on your device. This permission is used only to allow you to select images for puzzle creation. The app does not access any other files or data.

Data Storage

Any puzzles, progress, or preferences are stored locally on your device only. The app does not use cloud storage or remote servers.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use:

Advertising services

Analytics tools

Tracking technologies

Third-party SDKs that collect user data

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because no data is collected, the app is suitable for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Updates will be posted on this page.
